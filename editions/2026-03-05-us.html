Working Americans’ Tax Cut Act (WATCA) →

Senator Van Hollen’s proposal creates a cost-of-living tax exemption ($46K single / $64.4K head of household / $92K married filing jointly, phasing out at 175% of threshold) and adds a millionaire surtax (5% above $1M, 10% above $1.5M, 12% above income thresholds). Our new calculator lets you explore both provisions.
