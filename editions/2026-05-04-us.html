New in app v2 · Citations

Every parameter, traceable to its source

We just shipped citation surfacing in policyengine-app-v2—state-tax and benefit values now link back to the statute, regulation, or agency document they came from. Two states are live this week:

Hawaii

Hawaii household calculator →

Hawaii income tax, food/excise tax credit, and refundable EITC parameters now show their statutory references inline.

Vermont

Vermont household calculator →

Vermont income tax, CTC, renter credit, and child & dependent care credit values are sourced directly to Title 32 and Department of Taxes guidance.
