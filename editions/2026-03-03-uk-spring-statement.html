Key Finding

Most households see small income reductions by 2029

Despite slightly higher earnings growth in 2026, revised OBR forecasts translate to modest net income losses for most working-age households by 2029 — up to £34/year for couples with children. Pensioner couples are the only group with a small positive change.

Household Impact (2029)
