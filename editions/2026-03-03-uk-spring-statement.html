Our dashboard breaks down the Spring Statement 2026 across three views:

Forecast changes

Interactive charts comparing six economic indicators — earnings growth (+0.1pp), CPI inflation (−0.2pp), RPI inflation (−0.6pp), house prices (+0.2pp), per capita GDP (−0.4pp), and social rent (−0.1pp).

Population impact

Household income changes for six family types from 2026 to 2029. By 2029, most households see small reductions — up to £34/year for couples with children. Pensioner couples are the only group with a positive change (+£3).
