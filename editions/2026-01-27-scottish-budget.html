Key Finding

Net revenue of £196m in 2030-31

While the budget costs £64m in 2026-27, threshold freezes on higher earners generate growing revenue. In 2030-31, the package raises £196m net revenue for the Scottish Government.

Policy Measures

Basic and intermediate rate thresholds +7.4%

Scottish basic rate (20%) threshold rises to £16,538 and intermediate rate (21%) to £29,527. Working Scots keep more of their income before moving to higher tax bands.
