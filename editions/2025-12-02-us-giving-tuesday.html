GiveCalc 2.0: Calculate your true cost of giving

Happy Giving Tuesday! We've rebuilt GiveCalc from the ground up as a standalone web application at givecalc.org.

What's new in GiveCalc 2.0 →
  • New domain — Access directly at givecalc.org
  • All 50 states, plus DC and NYC — Federal and state tax savings in one calculation
  • 2025 tax year support — Calculate for 2024, 2025, or 2026, including HR1's charitable contribution changes
  • Expanded income types — Self-employment, capital gains, dividends, interest
  • Mobile-friendly — Use on any device

How it works

Enter your income, filing status, state, and deductions. GiveCalc shows:

  • Net tax impact — How much your donation reduces your tax liability
  • Marginal giving discount — Tax savings per additional dollar donated
  • Target donation finder — The donation to achieve a specific net income reduction
