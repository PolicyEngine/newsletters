Recent research from the PolicyEngine team

Carbon tax and dividend analysis

We examine how carbon tax and dividend policies affect UK households at £100 and £200 per tonne of CO2, with all revenue returned as equal per-person dividends.

Key finding:

71% of UK residents gain income under both scenarios

Read the full analysis →

VAT threshold impact study

Using synthetic firm microdata calibrated to ONS and HMRC statistics, we analyse how VAT threshold changes would affect revenues and businesses. The UK's current threshold is £90,000.

Key finding:

Raising the threshold to £100,000 would remove 48,000 firms from VAT registration and reduce revenue by £371 million in 2026-27.
