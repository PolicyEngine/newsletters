New Tool

Marriage incentive calculator

See how marriage affects your taxes, benefits, and credits in every US state.

Marriage can change your taxes, benefits, and credits in ways that aren't always obvious. Our new calculator brings it all together—federal and state income taxes, SNAP, Medicaid, ACA subsidies, the EITC, the CTC, and more—so you can see the net effect for your household.

Enter your household details, choose any state, and explore an interactive heatmap showing how marriage bonuses and penalties shift across the income spectrum. Click any point on the heatmap to see a detailed program-by-program breakdown.
