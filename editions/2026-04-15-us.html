New Interactive Tool

Cliff Watch: benefit cliffs, state by state

Cliff Watch traces a household's net income across an earnings curve and across all 50 states and DC. Build a household, watch the cliffs appear, and see where earning a dollar more leaves a family worse off.

New Dashboard

Working Parents Tax Relief Act

Rep. Kristen McDonald Rivet cited PolicyEngine in the release introducing the Working Parents Tax Relief Act, which boosts the EITC for families with children under 4. We built a companion dashboard to explore the reform's distributional, budgetary, and household-level impacts—including congressional-district breakdowns.

Explore the dashboard →

Research Experiment

What if income shifts from labor to capital?

A prototype experiment from our new AI inequality research track: we zero out positive labor income in 10-point steps, route the same $12.4T through existing positive capital income, and measure the distributional and fiscal effects under current law.
