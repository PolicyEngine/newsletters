ACA premium tax credits

The enhanced Affordable Care Act premium tax credits—at the heart of the current government shutdown fight—expire at the end of 2025. Our tool compares how enhanced versus original subsidy schedules affect any household configuration.

Calculate for your household:

  • Enter location (state and county), income, and household composition
  • Compare credit amounts under each scenario, reflecting your area's benchmark premiums
  • Understand how expiration could affect your coverage costs
