New Release

policyengine.py v4: one package, household to population

The new unified Python package gives researchers, developers, and policy analysts full programmatic access to the US tax-benefit model. Calculate household impacts with plain dictionaries, run population-level microsimulations with certified microdata from HuggingFace, and model custom reforms—all without the web interface.

import policyengine as pe

result = pe.us.calculate_household(
  household={...},
  output=["employment_income",
    "eitc", "household_net_income"]
)

New Interactive Tool

LIHEAP Benefit Calculator

LIHEAP helps low-income households pay heating and cooling bills with roughly $4 billion in annual federal block grants. Our new calculator estimates eligibility and benefit amounts for DC, Massachusetts, and Illinois—enter your state, income, household size, and heating source to see your estimated benefit.
